How To Use This Portal

The Billing Portal purpose is to help manage costs related to healthcare. A list of the most recent invoices can be found to the right of this paragraph. Additionally, functions to manage Insurance Claims, Insurance Data, Invoices, Payments and Statements are readily available to assist in organizing and managing this complext data.

It is important to note that this is a tool only to manage billing, invoices, payments, etc. on the patient side only. Data that you enter here is for your records only and cannot be viewed by third-party organizations. You must utilize the external links to make payments to these parties.
